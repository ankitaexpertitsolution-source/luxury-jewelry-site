Our Story

Crafted in the heart of Europe

Aurora Luxe Jewelry is a family atelier dedicated to the pursuit of brilliance. For over two decades, our master artisans have combined old-world techniques with modern design to create pieces that feel both timeless and distinctly personal.

Every diamond is conflict-free and hand-selected for fire, scintillation, and symmetry. Our metals are responsibly sourced, and each piece is hallmarked and accompanied by a certificate of authenticity.
